About Peach Outdoor

For all your outdoor equipment needs

Whether you're in the market for a new tractor, utility vehicle, zero turn mower, or other power equipment — Peach Outdoor is here for you. We're your go-to outdoor equipment supplier for Central Alabama, and one of the top-ranked Mahindra and LS Tractor dealers in the nation.

Our story

Founded in 2014, Peach Outdoor has spent over a decade helping Central Alabama find the right outdoor equipment for the job. Our owners, Mark and John, bring a lifetime of hands-on experience to the table — they don't just sell this equipment, they know it inside and out. That kind of real-world know-how is what sets us apart.

Our sales, service, parts, and finance departments are all here to help you find the right solution for your needs — whether you're a weekend warrior, a working farmer, or anything in between.
